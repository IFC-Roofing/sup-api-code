We charge a minimum 20% overhead and profit above the subcontractors cost as we act as the single point of contact for all subcontractors' trade complexities, plus cover all their liability and warranty for our clients.

Project Requires General Contracting
This project involves coordination of multiple trades—including roofing, gutter replacement, HVAC, chimney, metal work, skylight, pergola, and interior restoration. Per industry standards and Xactimate guidelines, O&P is warranted whenever three or more trades are involved and coordinated by a general contractor.

Complex Scheduling and Management
Each trade must be scheduled in the correct sequence, managed for quality and timing, and supervised for code compliance and safety. This requires administrative staff, project managers, and dedicated personnel which all reside at our physical office —all part of our company's operational infrastructure.

We Are Not a Single-Trade Contractor
Unlike subcontractors, we do not perform only one scope of work. We maintain a physical office, estimators, coordinators, insurance claim specialists, and field supervisors. These are all actual employees of IFC Contracting Solutions— these employees are the overhead which is built into O&P. These costs are necessary to support project execution, client communication, and warranty management.

Insurance Restoration Demands Administrative Oversight
Insurance jobs require extra documentation, invoicing, inspection scheduling, supplemental negotiation, and warranty coordination, all of which involve personnel beyond the jobsite. These employees reside at our physical location and work to adhere to the highest construction standards. This is not reimbursed in trade line items and is accounted for within O&P.
