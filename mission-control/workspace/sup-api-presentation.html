What We Built

A small service that translates between the IFC app and our supplement tools.

💬 Rep types in the supplement tab
↓
📡 IFC app sends request to our service
↓
⚙️ Sup AI runs the full pipeline
↓
📄 PDF generated + uploaded to Drive
↓
✅ Results appear right in the app

No new logins. No new tools. No new tabs.

SUP AI

Five Skills, One Tab

📋

"Generate a supplement"

Full pipeline — estimate, render, upload to Drive. Rep gets the link and dollar breakdown.

📊

"Run a bid markup"

Compares sub bids to Xactimate pricing. Flags discrepancies and markup issues.

📞

"Build a pre-call script"

Pulls full project context and builds a consultation prep guide.

☎️

"Give me a calling script"

Structured phone script for the adjuster call with all the context baked in.

🔍

"Review this supplement"

QA gate — verifies bids, measurements, photos, and F9 justifications.
